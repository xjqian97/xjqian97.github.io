About

I am a researcher specializing in storm surge dynamics, rainfall processes, and compound flooding under tropical cyclones. My research focuses on coastal disaster prevention and mitigation in the context of climate change.

Research Interests

  • Storm surge and coastal hydrodynamics under tropical cyclones
  • Wave modeling and wave–current coupling
  • Compound flooding in coastal and estuarine systems
  • Tropical cyclone wind, pressure, and rainfall modeling
  • Hydroclimate analysis in river basins
