About

I am a researcher specializing in storm surge dynamics, rainfall, and compound flooding under tropical cyclones. My research focuses on coastal disaster prevention and mitigation in the context of climate change.

Research Interests

  • Storm surge and coastal hydrodynamics under tropical cyclones
  • Wave modeling and wave–current coupling
  • Compound flooding
  • Tropical cyclones' wind, pressure and rainfall
  • Hydroclimate analysis in the river basins
