About

I am a coastal engineer specializing in storm surge dynamics, rainfall generation, and compound flooding under tropical cyclones. My research focuses on coastal disaster prevention and mitigation in the context of climate change.

Research Interests

  • Storm surge and coastal hydrodynamics under tropical cyclones
  • Wave modeling and wave–current coupling (SWAN, Delft3D-FM)
  • Compound flooding (storm surge, rainfall, river discharge)
  • Tropical cyclone rainfall modeling and risk assessment
  • Hydroclimate analysis in typhoon-prone basins

Selected Publications

  • Youn, J., Hwang, S., Qian, X., Im, S., Son, S.* (2026). Atmospheric Research. (RF-based TC pressure reconstruction)
